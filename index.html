FAQ

Direct answers for UAE and GCC decision makers.

Do new brands get branding and website building included in the marketing plan?
Yes. Launch System includes brand foundation and a 5-page conversion-first website build. This prevents wasted spend and inconsistent messaging. Launch activities only begin after the foundation is approved.
How do you prevent wasted spend on PPC?
We align targeting, keywords, negatives, creative, landing pages, and conversion tracking. Optimization is weekly, and reporting explains changes and next steps.
What is the minimum commitment?
Retainers are 3 months minimum. This allows time for diagnosis, fixes, baselining, and controlled scaling. One-time projects are scoped with delivery timelines.
How do you report results?
Reporting is monthly and includes outcomes, insights, what changed, why it matters, and what we do next month. We add a testing plan so performance improves by design.
